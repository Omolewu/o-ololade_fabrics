Frequently Asked Questions

Need answers? Find them here...

Is it possible for me to pick up my online order from your local store?

• Yes, all orders placed on www.o-ololadefabrics.com are processed and available for pickup at our store (Shop D165 Qudus line New GBAGI Market).

• It can also be delivered via our local bus service; we send items from a motor park and deliver them to the customer's nearest motor park; however, we only send goods within Ibadan if the customer can pay their private logistics.

• This means you can pick up your order at our store.

How long will it take for my order to be processed?

• We strive to complete all orders within two business days.

• It may take up to three business days to complete orders received during a sale or promotional period.

• If your order is urgent, please email contact@o-ololadefabrics.com and we will do everything we can to complete it the same day.

How do you measure fabrics for my order?

• All orders are measured in inches per yard or per trouser, with 0.25-inch increments, and we will do our best to offer your fabric in one continuous piece unless otherwise noted for remnants.

• We work in 36-inch-per-yard and 45-inch-per-trouser increments.

• Units are reflected on some website pages.

Can I edit, add to, or cancel an order that has already been placed?

• No, once an order is placed and the items are paid for, the order is final and no changes can be made.

• If you need to contact us about a recent order, please email azeezyuscom@gmail.com or call +234 813 458 7119 as soon as possible, specifying your order number, and we will respond as soon as possible.

Is it necessary for me to create an account to shop online?

• To purchase from the online store, you do not need to create an account.

• You can track your package, check previous orders, and save information so that forms are pre-filled for future transactions if you create an account.

In what currency will I be billed?

• In Nigeria, the naira is the default currency.

Which payment methods do you accept?

• We do not take debit or credit cards from Visa or Mastercard.

• We can accept payment by transfer, but you must notify us of your payment immediately.

Is there someone I can talk to about general questions?

• Yes, you can contact us by email at azeezyuscom@gmail.com or by phone at +234 813 458 7119 if you have any questions.

How can I find out when new fabrics are available in the store?

• If you join the mailing list, you'll be the first to hear about new arrivals.
